Republic of Jiu Jitsu

About

Republic of Jiu Jitsu is a long established Brazilian Jiu Jitsu club based on Pearse Street, being based very closely to Pearse Dart Station and the Trinity College campus. They offer fundamental classes in Gi (BJJ uniform) and No-Gi (sports shirt/rashguard and shorts) where you will learn and repeat techniques in a friendly, safe and professional environment. Advanced level classes are also on offer which are geared more towards competition and sparring.

Contact

13 Magennis Place
Dublin 2

Jungle BJJ Dublin

About

Jungle BJJ Dublin originally opened its doors in 2016 in Smithfield and has since relocated to new premises in the Liberties, Dublin 8, just a few minutes walk from St Patricks Cathedral and Christchurch. They offer classes available to both total beginners and experienced grapplers which are scheduled throughout the day. The core principle behind Jungle BJJ can be expressed in one simple phrase: Train hard and have fun! Here you can expect high-quality training and a friendly and welcoming atmostphere.

Contact

67 - 70 Meath Street
Dublin 8

JS Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Academy

About

JSBJJ is a well-established team in Dublin that has been growing since 2011. They are based on Parnell Street in Dublin City Center, making the club highly accessible and well-located. The main philosphy of JSBJJ is that BJJ is for everyone, but everyone is different. Therefore, they tailor Jiu Jitsu for each of their members and take good care ensuring their students are constantly progressing. They offer classes at both beginner and advanced levels, taught by coaches that are driven by a passion for martial arts, in and out of the dojo.

Contact

189/193 Parnell Street
Dublin 1
