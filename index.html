Why train BJJ?

Improve your problem-solving skills

Think of BJJ as a pysical game of chess. In chess, you cannot make a calculated move without anticipating your opponents next step. This makes BJJ not only a workout for the body, but also the mind! Regular training not only enhances your problem-solving skills, but also your ability to operate under pressure.

Learn Self Defence

The fundamentals of BJJ are well-rounded instructions on excellent self-defence mechanisms. Where other martial arts focus on punching and kicking, the fundamentals of BJJ teach us that the most effective way to defend yourself is to close distance and control your opponent.

Join an encouraging and supportive community

BJJ is not just a sport, for many it is a lifestyle! It is incredibly fun and appeals to everyone in all walks of life. Not only is it addictive, its fun!

Build strength and fitness

Learning BJJ involves fun and high-intensity pyhsical activity. You will notice your cardio, strength and endurance improve a great deal!
